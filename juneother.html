Facts about World Sickle Cell Day

Fact 1: Sickle Cell Anemia is caused by inheriting a gene from each parent that have abnormal code hemoglibin S "Hgb S" this caused your red blood cells to become sickle shaped and rigid.

Fact 2: Sickle Cell Disease around 100,000 people are diagnosed with SCD yearly, and occurs in 1 out of 365 African Americans.
